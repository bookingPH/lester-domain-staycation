Booking Policy

Please read before making a reservation.

Reservation Fee

A non-refundable reservation fee is required to confirm your booking. This fee will be deducted from your total balance upon check-in. The remaining balance is due upon arrival.

How to Reserve

  • Select your preferred room and dates on the booking page
  • Fill in your guest details
  • Choose any add-ons you want
  • Send the reservation fee via GCash, PayMaya, or bank transfer
  • Upload your payment screenshot
  • Wait for confirmation via email (usually within 1–2 hours)

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be accommodated subject to availability — please contact us in advance

Guest Count

Each room has a maximum guest capacity. Additional guests beyond the base capacity are charged an extra guest fee per night. Exceeding the maximum capacity is not allowed.

House Rules

  • No smoking inside the property
  • Quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM
  • Pets are only allowed in pet-friendly rooms (additional fee applies)
  • Guests are responsible for any damages to the property
  • Please keep the property clean and dispose of trash properly

Payment Methods

  • GCash
  • PayMaya
  • Bank Transfer

Cash payments upon check-in are accepted for the remaining balance only. The reservation fee must be paid digitally.

Confirmation

Bookings are confirmed only after your payment screenshot has been verified by our team. You will receive a confirmation email within 1–2 hours of submitting your booking.
